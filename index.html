Ecological Pyramids

This is a simulation to help you better understand ecological pyramids.

Using this simulation, you can generate the pyramid of numbers and pyramid of biomass
based on an ecosystem composition of your choice.

Using the field below, indicate the number of species present in this ecosystem. Then, add in the
abundance (no. of individuals) and biomass (mass of each individual in kilograms) for each species.

*Note that ecological pyramids are arranged such that Producers are always found at the base of the pyramids.

After selecting the number of species, followed by the abundance and biomass of the species,
click 'Generate Ecosystem' to obtain the plots.
